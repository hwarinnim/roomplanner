How to Use Room Planner:

1. Set your room size.
Enter your room's width and height in cm to create the space.

2. Add your furniture.
Use squares/rectangles for most items and circles/ovals for round ones.

3. Adjust if needed.
Tap on View Items to edit your furniture. Rotate items to fit how they really sit in your space.

4. Arrange your layout.
Drag items into place.

5. Undo / Redo / Reset / Clear.
Fix mistakes or start fresh anytime.

6. Save your layout.
Save as an HTML file to keep a copy of your plan. Only opens on desktop.
